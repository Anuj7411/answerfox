   - `listMonthlyAiFixUsage` (in `lib/db/queries/ai-fixes.ts`)

4. **Server Action** (`apps/web/src/app/(dashboard)/dashboard/sites/[siteId]/ai-fix-actions.ts`):
   - `generateAIFixAction(findingId)` returns discriminated `GenerateAiFixState`
   - States: idle | succeeded | quota-exceeded | failed
